# Data Notice

This repository includes research materials used in the accompanying paper,
including:

- a cleaned 9000-case first-instance corpus
- framework prediction outputs on that corpus
- 100-case human blind-audit materials
- 100-case prompt-based AI blind-audit outputs and derived metrics

These materials are shared for transparency, research inspection, and
reproducibility of the paper. Users remain responsible for checking the legal,
ethical, and institutional requirements that apply in their own jurisdiction or
use case before redistributing, republishing, or using the data for other
purposes.

Nothing in this repository should be read as legal advice or as a claim that
all included source materials are free of every downstream restriction in every
jurisdiction.
